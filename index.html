Who am I?

M. Eng. Andrzej Globisz

I finished mechatronics studies on Silesian University of Technology in Gliwice, Electrical Department. During the course of studies I have learnt solid basics of programming in variety of environments and technologies (assembler, C, C++, C#, Matlab/Simulink, LabVIEW). It supplied me with fair amount of knowledge about object oriented programming. Apart from that I was learning HTML, CSS, Java, Javascript and other related Front-end tools.

During studies I started working as Control Systems Engineer in large publicly traded engineering, construction, and project management companies. It allowed me to develop client relations skills, ability to cooperate in the project team and urge for quality of my work.


Programming has always been my hobby, something that I am passionate about. I want to start a new chapter in my professional career as Junior Front-end Developer.
